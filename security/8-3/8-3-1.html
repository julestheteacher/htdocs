Threat Mitigation Strategies

Click each category below to explore mitigation techniques:

  • Hardware: Configure BIOS/UEFI settings, disable unused ports, enable secure boot.
  • Software: Apply least privilege principles, configure firewalls, enforce secure defaults.
  • Function: Detect and remove malicious software.
  • Actions: Real-time scanning, quarantine infected files, automatic updates.

Monitors network and system activities for malicious actions or policy violations.

  • Hashing: One-way encryption for data integrity.
  • Symmetric: Same key for encryption and decryption.
  • Asymmetric: Public and private key pair for secure communication.

Define roles, permissions, and authentication requirements to prevent unauthorized access.

Background checks and verification before granting access to sensitive systems.

Regular cybersecurity awareness programs to reduce human error and social engineering risks.

Implement ACLs (Access Control Lists) and RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) in applications.

Disable unnecessary services, apply patches, and enforce strong configurations.

  • Types: Full, incremental, differential backups.
  • Safe Storage: Encrypt backups and store off-site or in secure cloud environments.

Regular patching to fix vulnerabilities and improve security.

Keep device firmware and drivers updated to prevent exploitation of hardware-level vulnerabilities.

Physically isolate critical systems from external networks to prevent remote attacks.

Ensure APIs are secure and verified to prevent unauthorized data exchange.

Encrypt network traffic to protect data in transit and prevent interception.

Add extra layers of security beyond passwords, such as OTPs or biometrics.

Securely store and generate strong passwords to reduce credential reuse risks.

Identify open ports and close unnecessary ones to reduce attack surface.

  • Ethical Hacking: Authorized testing to find vulnerabilities before attackers do.
  • Unethical Hacking: Malicious exploitation of vulnerabilities for illegal purposes.
