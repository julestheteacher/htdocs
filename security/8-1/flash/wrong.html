8.2.3 Human Vulnerabilities

Explanation of Human Threats

Click each category below to expand and view details:

  • Salaries and benefits: Disclosure can cause internal disputes and targeted attacks.
  • Staff personal details: Includes addresses and IDs; leaks can lead to identity theft.
  • Client details: Exposure damages trust and can lead to legal issues.
  • Stakeholder details: Exploited for fraud or insider trading.
  • Intellectual property: Loss harms competitiveness.
  • Sales numbers: Competitors may use leaked figures.
  • Contracts: Unauthorized access can lead to manipulation.
  • Usernames and passwords: Basic credentials are prime targets.
  • MFA details: If compromised, MFA loses its security advantage.
  • PIN and access codes: Used for banking or secure systems.
  • Biometric data: Cannot be changed like passwords, making breaches permanent.
