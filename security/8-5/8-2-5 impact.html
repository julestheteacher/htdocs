8.2 Impact of Threats and Vulnerabilities

Explanation of Threats

Click each category below to understand the potential impact on an organisation:

When sensitive data such as customer details, financial records, or intellectual property is leaked or lost, it can lead to:

  • Breach of privacy laws and regulations.
  • Financial penalties and compensation claims.
  • Severe reputational damage and loss of trust.

Hackers gaining access to systems can result in:

  • Theft or manipulation of data.
  • Installation of malware or ransomware.
  • Disruption of business operations.

Corrupted data can lead to:

  • Loss of critical business information.
  • Increased recovery time and costs.
  • Operational inefficiencies and decision-making errors.

Interruptions to services can cause:

  • Downtime leading to lost productivity.
  • Financial losses due to halted operations.
  • Customer dissatisfaction and churn.

Physical breaches can result in:

  • Theft of equipment or sensitive documents.
  • Damage to infrastructure.
  • Increased risk of insider threats.
